#GTNP

Grand Teton National Park (#GTNP) is a 309,994 acre national park outside of Jackson, Wyoming. The park was established on February 26, 1929, and it attracts thousands of hikers, skiiers and mountain climbers each year as a result of the beauty and athletic challenge it represents. Teton peaks soar 7,000 feet above the surrounding valley, and the Snake River winds by the mountains through the park.
